PDPA Notice

1. Introduction

This Personal Data Protection Notice ("Notice") is issued by DSA Development Sdn. Bhd. ("Company", "we", "our", or "us") in accordance with the Personal Data Protection Act 2010 Malaysia.

This Notice explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal data in connection with our property development business.

2. Collection of Personal Data

We may collect and process personal data from you, including:

  • Full name, NRIC/passport number
  • Contact details (address, phone number, email)
  • Demographic information (age, gender, nationality)
  • Employment and income details
  • Banking and financial information
  • Property preferences and transaction history

Your personal data may be collected through:

  • Booking forms and sale & purchase agreements
  • Website registrations and inquiries
  • Property exhibitions and events
  • Communications via phone, email, or messaging platforms

3. Purpose of Processing

Your personal data is collected and processed for purposes including:

  • Processing property purchases, bookings, and transactions
  • Facilitating financing or loan applications with financial institutions
  • Providing updates on property projects and services
  • Responding to inquiries and providing customer support
  • Internal record keeping and administrative purposes
  • Compliance with legal and regulatory obligations

4. Disclosure of Personal Data

Your personal data may be disclosed to:

  • Banks and financial institutions
  • Lawyers, valuers, and consultants
  • Government authorities and regulatory bodies
  • Contractors, service providers, and business partners
  • Marketing agencies (where consent is given)

5. Data Security

We are committed to ensuring that your personal data is secure. We implement appropriate administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

6. Retention of Personal Data

Your personal data will be retained only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes stated in this Notice or as required by law.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications

Requests can be made by contacting us using the details below.

8. Failure to Provide Personal Data

Failure to provide required personal data may result in our inability to:

  • Process your property purchase or booking
  • Provide requested services or information

9. Transfer of Personal Data

Your personal data may be transferred to locations outside Malaysia where necessary, subject to appropriate safeguards.

10. Changes to This Notice

We reserve the right to update this Notice from time to time. Any changes will be communicated via our website or other appropriate channels.
