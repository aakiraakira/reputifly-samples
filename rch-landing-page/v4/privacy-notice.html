Privacy Notice

1. Introduction

DSA Development Sdn. Bhd. ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your personal data in accordance with applicable laws such as the Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA) in Malaysia.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information when you interact with us, including when you purchase property, visit our website, or engage with our services.

2. Personal Data We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal data:

  • Identity Information: Name, NRIC/passport number, date of birth
  • Contact Information: Address, phone number, email
  • Financial Information: Income details, bank account information, loan/financing details
  • Property Preferences: Type of property, budget, location interests
  • Transaction Data: Purchase history, booking details
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type (for website users)

3. How We Collect Data

We collect personal data through:

  • Property bookings and sales agreements
  • Registration forms (online/offline)
  • Marketing events and exhibitions
  • Website forms and cookies
  • Communications via email, phone, or messaging apps

4. Purpose of Processing Personal Data

We use your personal data to:

  • Process property purchases and transactions
  • Facilitate loan/financing applications
  • Communicate project updates and offers
  • Provide customer support
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements
  • Improve our services and marketing strategies

5. Disclosure of Personal Data

We may share your data with:

  • Financial institutions (banks, mortgage providers)
  • Legal firms and valuers
  • Government authorities and regulators
  • Contractors and service providers
  • Marketing partners (with consent where required)

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law.

8. Your Rights

Under applicable laws, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications
  • Request deletion (subject to legal limitations)

To exercise your rights, please contact us using the details below.
