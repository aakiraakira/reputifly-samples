Decking Installation

Elevate the aesthetic and value of your home with our bespoke timber and composite decking solutions. Engineered for durability.

Natural Timber Decking

Nothing beats the rich, authentic warmth of true timber. We handle premium woods like Chengal, Teak, and Merbau, fully treated and professionally oiled to ensure longevity and weather resistance.

  • Authentic aesthetic appeal
  • Treated for pest resistance
  • Premium sourcing (Chengal/Teak)

Composite Decking (WPC)

For those seeking a zero-maintenance finish, our advanced composite decking is the ideal choice. Highly durable against UV decay, warping, and splinters — perfect for poolside and sunny rooftop applications.

  • Zero splinters or warping
  • Ultra-low maintenance
  • Sustainable materials
