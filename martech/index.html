Hello
MarTech Lovers!

We are a non-profit community of marketing, data, and technology professionals in APAC, started in Singapore.

Jana and Reed were having a long-awaited drink, swapping stories about our adventures in the martech world and it felt like therapy. A brilliant idea struck them—why not create an informal group to share these experiences?

They reached out to their network and launched the We Love MarTech group in December 2023. Here we are, continuously growing and connecting more professionals on our journey.

Our Mission

We are dedicated to creating a vibrant and inclusive platform with four core directives.

🔗

Connect

Bringing together industry leaders to share insights, experiences, and bleeding-edge practices in a collaborative environment.

📈

Promote

Fostering continuous learning through peer-to-peer knowledge sharing, specialized training, and exclusive workshops.

💡

Inspire

Driving technology and data-driven marketing forward by encouraging innovative thinking and unconventional solutions.

🤝

Support

Aiding personal and professional development with curated resources, mentorship, and high-value networking opportunities.
