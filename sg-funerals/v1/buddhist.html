Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a Buddhist Funeral package cost?
+
Our Buddhist funeral packages start from $5,288 Nett. The price includes the 3-day wake setup, casket, embalming, and monk chanting.
Why is vegetarian food required at the wake?
+
In Buddhist tradition, serving vegetarian food prevents the creation of negative karma associated with killing animals, aiding the deceased in a favorable rebirth.
What should guests wear?
+
Guests should dress in modest attire. White, black, or blue are acceptable. Bright red should be avoided as it is considered a celebratory color.
Do you handle the location booking?
+
Yes, our package includes the coordination and booking of the HDB Void Deck or Funeral Parlour, as well as the cremation slot booking at Mandai or other crematoriums.
Are payment plans available?
+
We practice transparent billing. A deposit is usually required upon confirmation, with the balance due after the funeral. We accept PayNow, Bank Transfer, and Cheques.
