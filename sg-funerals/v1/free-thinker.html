Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a Free Thinker Funeral cost?
+
Free thinker funerals are generally more affordable than religious ones due to the absence of priests and complex rituals. Our standard 3-day package starts at $4,360 Nett.
Can we customize the program?
+
Absolutely. Since there are no hard rules, it is "free-play". You can incorporate favorite music, specific dress codes, or unique memorial activities. We take effort to understand the decedent to conduct a funeral in his or her taste.
Can we still have burning offerings?
+
Yes. Some families identify as free-thinkers but still wish to follow certain cultural Chinese traditions like burning paper offerings. We can arrange for this upon request.
Do you handle the location booking?
+
Yes, our package includes the coordination and booking of the HDB Void Deck or Funeral Parlour, as well as the cremation slot booking at Mandai or other crematoriums.
Are payment plans available?
+
We practice transparent billing. A deposit is usually required upon confirmation, with the balance due after the funeral. We accept PayNow, Bank Transfer, and Cheques.
