Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What should I do immediately when death occurs?
+
If at home, call a doctor to certify death. If at the hospital, the doctor will issue the documents. Once you have the Death Certificate, call us at 8824 1010 to engage our services for body collection.
What is the difference between HDB Void Deck and Parlour wakes?
+
HDB Void Deck wakes are convenient for residents and offer a community atmosphere, but require tentage setup. Funeral Parlours provide air-conditioned comfort and privacy but may be less accessible for some guests. We can arrange for either.
Do you offer Direct Cremation services?
+
Yes, we offer Direct Cremation (Straight Case) packages which include the casket, transportation, and cremation booking without a wake. This is a dignified and affordable option for families who prefer simplicity.
Can you arrange for Sea Burial?
+
Yes, after cremation, we can assist with the Ash Scattering at Sea ceremony at the designated site south of Pulau Semakau. We provide the boat charter and necessary biodegradable urns.
