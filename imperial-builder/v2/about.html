Our Story & Mission

Founded in 2010, Imperial Builder PTE LTD began with a singular vision: to elevate Singapore's renovation landscape with uncompromising quality and expert craftsmanship. What started as a small team of passionate renovation specialists has grown into a full-service renovation firm trusted by homeowners and businesses across the island.

Our mission is simple — to deliver renovation excellence that transforms spaces and exceeds expectations. Every project we undertake reflects our commitment to integrity, innovation, and the highest standards of workmanship.

Our Vision & Philosophy

Our Vision

To be Singapore's most trusted and admired renovation firm — recognized for transforming homes and commercial spaces into inspiring environments. We aspire to set the gold standard in residential and commercial renovation through innovative design, sustainable practices, and relentless pursuit of perfection.

Our Philosophy

We believe that great renovation is the intersection of design vision and construction expertise. Every space we transform must not only function flawlessly but also reflect the personality and aspirations of our clients. We prioritize lasting relationships over transactions, treating every client's renovation as if it were our own.

Our Expertise & Approach

What sets Imperial Builder apart is our integrated renovation approach. We don't just renovate — we consult, design, engineer, and manage. Every renovation benefits from our multi-disciplinary expertise and meticulous attention to detail.

Our methodology combines proven renovation techniques with modern design tools, ensuring every project is structurally sound, aesthetically refined, and delivered on time.
