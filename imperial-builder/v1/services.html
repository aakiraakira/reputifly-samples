Crafting Spaces That Define Excellence

Imperial Builder offers a comprehensive range of construction, design, and project management services. Whether you're envisioning a luxury landed home, transforming a commercial space, or seeking a seamless design & build experience — our team delivers with precision and artistry.

Every project is guided by our commitment to quality materials, transparent communication, and on-time delivery. Explore our services below to discover how we can bring your vision to life.

Residential Solutions

Specialized services tailored for homeowners, from luxury landed property rebuilds to comprehensive interior renovations. We navigate complex regulatory approvals and deliver bespoke residential masterpieces.

  • Landed property complete rebuilds
  • HDB & condo interior renovations
  • Luxury kitchen & bathroom remodeling
  • Custom carpentry & built-in solutions

Commercial Solutions

Expert services designed for businesses and commercial developments. We transform workspaces into functional, brand-aligned environments while minimizing operational downtime.

  • Office fit-outs & renovations
  • Retail & F&B interior build-outs
  • Commercial property upgrades
  • Brand-aligned workspace design

Interior Design

Creative and functional interior planning that prioritizes both aesthetics and usability. Our design team creates spaces that reflect your personality and lifestyle, down to the finest material selections.

  • Space planning & conceptual design
  • Material & finish consultation
  • 3D visualization & rendering
  • Furniture sourcing & styling

Renovation Services

Comprehensive remodeling and upgrading solutions for various spaces. Whether it's a complete overhaul or targeted upgrades, we enhance both functionality and visual appeal.

  • Full-home & partial renovations
  • Structural modifications
  • Electrical & plumbing overhauls
  • Feature wall & ceiling treatments

Full Project Management

End-to-end oversight and coordination of complex projects. Our dedicated project managers ensure timely delivery, budget control, and quality assurance at every phase.

  • Budget planning & cost control
  • Vendor & subcontractor coordination
  • Timeline & milestone management
  • Quality inspections & compliance

A&A Works (Additions & Alterations)

Modifying and expanding existing structures to meet evolving needs. We handle all BCA approvals and structural engineering requirements for a seamless expansion experience.

  • Structural extensions & additions
  • Roof & attic conversions
  • Basement construction
  • BCA submission & approval handling

Design & Build

An integrated service offering seamless design and construction from concept to completion under one roof. This eliminates miscommunication between designers and builders, delivering a cohesive result.

  • Single-point accountability
  • Concept-to-completion delivery
  • Faster timelines & cost efficiency
  • Unified design & construction teams
