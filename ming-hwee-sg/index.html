Frequently Asked

Answers to the Questions Families Ask Most

Everything you need to know about hiring a domestic helper in Singapore with Ming Hwee.

To hire a foreign domestic helper, you must be at least 21 years old, able to provide for the helper's upkeep and maintenance, and live in an eligible residence (HDB, condo or landed). You also need to meet the minimum monthly household income requirement set by MOM. Our team verifies all eligibility criteria during your free consultation.

All helpers we place complete government-mandated training before starting work. First-time helpers attend the Settling-In Programme (SIP) upon arrival in Singapore. Our partner training centres also provide specialised modules in childcare, eldercare, cooking and household management as required by each family.

From consultation to placement, most Filipino and Indonesian placements take 6–10 weeks, while Myanmar placements can be faster at 3–5 weeks due to streamlined processing. Transfer helpers already in Singapore can start within 1–2 weeks after work permit approval.

We offer a replacement guarantee within the first 6 months. If the match is not suitable after reasonable effort and mediation, we will present a new curated shortlist at no additional agency fee. Our goal is a long-term, happy placement — not a one-time transaction.

By law, every employer must purchase medical insurance (minimum S$15,000 per year) and personal accident insurance (minimum S$60,000 per year) for their helper. Additionally, a S$5,000 security bond is required for non-Malaysian helpers, typically covered by an insurable bond of S$60–80 per year.

The concessionary levy of S$60 per month (instead of the standard S$300) applies if your household has a child under 16, an elderly family member aged 67+, or a family member with disabilities. We help verify eligibility and submit the concessionary levy application to MOM on your behalf.

Helper work permits are valid for up to 2 years and must be renewed before expiry. Ming Hwee handles the entire renewal process, including medical check-ups, insurance renewal, bond continuation, and MOM submission — so you can focus on your family.

Our agency fees vary based on the helper's origin country, experience level, and specific services required. You only pay our agency fee upon successful placement after the helper begins work. Use the Singpass-powered portal to get a transparent, personalised quote before you commit.
