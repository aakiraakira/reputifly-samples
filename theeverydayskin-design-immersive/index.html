Questions

Frequently Asked

Is the procedure painful?

Most clients find it very tolerable. We apply a strong numbing cream beforehand, so you will likely only feel a slight "ant-bite" sensation or heat. It is much less painful than manual extraction.

Will it leave scars?

Scarring is extremely rare with our technique. You will have small scabs for a few days—this is normal! As long as you do not pick the scabs and let them fall off naturally, your skin will heal smoothly.

How long is the downtime?

You can return to work immediately. The treated spots will look like tiny brown dots (scabs) which fall off in 3 to 7 days. We recommend avoiding makeup on the area for 24 hours.
