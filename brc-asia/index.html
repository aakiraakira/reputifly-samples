Our Geographical Presence

An ever-growing footprint of iconic landmarks that feature our steel reinforcing solutions are testament to the safety and build quality that BRC Asia has come to be associated with.

This growing list includes iconic railway projects in Shanghai and Beijing, upscale resorts in the Maldives, and landmark buildings in Singapore such as Marina Bay Sands and Pinnacle@Duxton – the world’s tallest residential project.
