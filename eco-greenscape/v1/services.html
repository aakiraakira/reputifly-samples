Our Services

Comprehensive, eco-friendly landscaping and maintenance solutions for residential and commercial properties.

Expertise You Can Trust

At Eco Greenscape, we blend horticultural science with artistic design to offer a full spectrum of landscaping services. Whether you need a complete garden overhaul or regular maintenance to keep your lawn pristine, our sustainable approach guarantees a healthy, visually stunning result that respects the local environment.

Lawn Care & Maintenance

Lush, green, and sustainably managed.

A vibrant lawn is the foundation of any great landscape. Our turf experts utilize organic fertilization, precise mowing techniques, and strategic aeration to promote deep root growth and drought resistance. We also provide safe, eco-friendly pest and weed management, ensuring your lawn is safe for children, pets, and local wildlife.

  • Organic turf fertilization
  • Precision mowing and edging
  • Aeration and overseeding

Garden Design & Installation

Bringing your vision to life.

Our landscape architects work closely with you to design a custom outdoor space that complements your property's architecture and suits your lifestyle. From selecting native, drought-tolerant plants to constructing elegant hardscaping and soothing water features, we manage the entire installation process with meticulous attention to detail.

  • 3D Landscape Design
  • Native & Tropical Plant Sourcing
  • Hardscaping & Paths

Tree & Shrub Care

Protecting your landscape's biggest assets.

Trees and shrubs require specialized care to maintain their structural integrity and health. Our certified arborists provide expert structural pruning, disease diagnostics, and targeted deep-root feeding. We proactively manage the health of your woody plants to prevent issues before they start, ensuring they provide shade and beauty for decades.

  • Structural pruning & shaping
  • Disease & pest diagnostics
  • Deep root fertilization

Irrigation Systems

Smart watering for sustainable growth.

Water is a precious resource. We design and install state-of-the-art "smart" irrigation systems that minimize runoff and maximize absorption. By utilizing drip irrigation and weather-sensing technology, we ensure your landscape receives exactly the moisture it needs to thrive, lowering your water bills and protecting the environment.

  • Smart sensor installation
  • Drip irrigation setup
  • System repair and winterization
