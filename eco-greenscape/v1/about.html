About Ecogreenscape

Cultivating beautiful, sustainable environments in Singapore through expert landscaping and an unwavering commitment to nature.

Our Roots & Vision

Ecogreenscape was founded with a singular vision: to merge aesthetic brilliance with ecological responsibility. What began as a small team of passionate horticulturists has blossomed into Singapore's premier eco-conscious landscaping firm.

We believe that outdoor spaces are extensions of our homes and businesses, and they should be nurtured in harmony with the environment. For years, we have pushed the boundaries of traditional landscaping by prioritizing organic practices, water conservation, and native planting.

Our Mission

To design, install, and maintain lush environments that elevate our clients' quality of life while actively protecting and enriching the local ecosystem.

Our Philosophy & Values

The core principles that guide every design we draft, every seed we plant, and every client we serve.

Sustainability First

We prioritize organic fertilizers, zero-emission tools where possible, and water-smart irrigation to ensure our footprint remains light and green.

Uncompromising Quality

From the sourcing of robust plants to the precision of our hardscaping, we hold ourselves to the highest standards of craftsmanship.

Client Collaboration

We listen closely. Your vision combined with our expertise guarantees an outdoor space that perfectly reflects your lifestyle or brand.

Growing Together: Our Community Impact

Ecogreenscape is deeply rooted in the Singaporean community. We believe that an eco-friendly future requires education and collective action.

Through our "Green Tomorrow" initiative, our staff routinely volunteers for local park clean-ups and urban tree planting drives. Additionally, we host free bi-monthly workshops for residential neighborhoods, teaching locals how to start their own organic urban gardens and compost systems.

When you hire Ecogreenscape, a portion of our proceeds goes directly into funding green spaces in underserved urban areas.
