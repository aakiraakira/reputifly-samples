About Us

Discover our roots, our mission, and the passionate team behind Eco Greenscape.

Our Story & Mission

Founded with a deep reverence for nature, Eco Greenscape began as a small initiative to bring sustainable garden maintenance to local neighborhoods. Over the years, our dedication to eco-friendly practices and exceptional service has allowed us to grow into a premier landscaping firm in Singapore.

Our Mission: To transform ordinary spaces into thriving, sustainable eco-havens that enhance the well-being of our clients and the environment. We believe that beautiful landscaping should not come at the expense of our planet.

"We don't just landscape; we cultivate living ecosystems that stand the test of time."

Giving Back to Our Roots

We believe a business should enrich its community. Every year, Eco Greenscape commits a portion of our resources and volunteer hours to local community garden restoration and urban planting initiatives across Singapore.

By partnering with local schools to create educational green spaces, we're helping plant the seeds of environmental stewardship in the next generation.
