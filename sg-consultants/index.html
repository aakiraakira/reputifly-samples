Frequently Asked Questions

What regions do you serve?

We have a strong presence across Southeast Asia, with established headquarters in Singapore and key regional offices located in Hanoi and Ho Chi Minh City (Vietnam), as well as Bangkok (Thailand).

What does "Sustainahaus" mean?

Sustainahaus is our proprietary approach to integrating sustainable, eco-friendly practices into building design and construction. It focuses on energy efficiency, reduced carbon footprints, and creating spaces that promote long-term environmental health.

Do you handle both design and construction?

Yes, through our "SG Design & Build" service, we offer comprehensive end-to-end solutions. This allows us to maintain strict quality control, streamline communication, and deliver projects on time and within budget.

How does your Investment Advisory service work?

Our investment advisory team provides strategic insights into regional property markets, risk assessment, portfolio management, and feasibility studies to ensure your capital is deployed effectively for maximum ROI.
