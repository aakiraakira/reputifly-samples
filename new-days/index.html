Peace of Mind for Your Family

As we wear more hats in life, it becomes an increasingly difficult strain for us to find time to take care of our children or elderly loved ones. If it seems like you have overstretched, perhaps it is time to let New Days Housekeeper lend a helping hand.

The agency believes in matching the right domestic helper according to each family’s household requirements. We boast a team of carefully-screened helpers direct from our overseas training centers that have relevant qualifications and working experiences.

Migrant domestic workers leave their families to work here in Singapore to give their loved ones better lives. Many become strong pillars of support for our families with their dedication and hard work.
