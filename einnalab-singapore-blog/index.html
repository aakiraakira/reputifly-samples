HSA Compliance Notice (Singapore Health Sciences Authority) All 100 blog posts in this calendar are designed to comply with Singapore's strict supplement advertising guidelines. No post makes disease-treatment or cure claims. Content uses evidence-based framing ("what research suggests", "what studies indicate") and avoids prohibited terms like "cure", "treat", "prevent disease", or "remedy". Every post must include a standard HSA disclaimer: "This product is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease." Competitor comparison content must remain factual and avoid disparagement. All health claims must cite peer-reviewed sources.
