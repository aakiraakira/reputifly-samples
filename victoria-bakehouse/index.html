A Tale of Two Cities

Born from a passion for classic Parisian baking and the bustling energy of Hong Kong's street markets, we craft unique, handcrafted bakes that bridge two culinary worlds.

Our kitchen is a playground of flavor, where butter-rich laminations meet classic cha chaan teng staples. Every bite tells a story of heritage, travel, and uncompromising quality.

Our Signature Bakes

Discover the pillars of our unique bakery concept.

French Classics

Flaky, buttery, and traditionally Parisian. Croissants, pain au chocolat, and delicate tartes made with imported AOP butter.

Hong Kong Heritage

The Fusions
