Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about getting started at Elevate.

What age can my child start rhythmic gymnastics?

We welcome students starting at age 4 for our recreational introductory classes. At this age, the focus is on developing coordination, basic flexibility, and a love for movement in a fun environment.

Do we need to buy apparatus (ribbons, hoops) immediately?

No, the academy provides all necessary apparatus for beginners and recreational classes. If your child progresses to the competitive team, coaches will advise you on purchasing personalized, competition-grade equipment.

How do I know if my child is ready for the competitive team?

Competitive team placement is by invitation or evaluation only. Our coaches conduct regular assessments during recreational classes. If you are transferring from another gym, you can schedule a private evaluation via our contact form.

What should my child wear to class?

Gymnasts should wear form-fitting athletic wear, such as a leotard or a tight tank top and leggings, so coaches can properly observe body alignment. Hair must be tied back securely, and bare feet or rhythmic half-shoes are required.
