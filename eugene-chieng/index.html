My Approach

A Process Built
on Precision

Every client engagement follows a rigorous four-stage framework refined over 15 years and hundreds of successful outcomes.

1

Deep Financial Discovery

I begin with a comprehensive analysis of your current financial position, property holdings, CPF balances, loan eligibility, and long-term goals. No assumptions -- only data.

2

Multi-Move Strategy Design

Using institutional-grade modelling, I map out your next 2-3 property moves. Every scenario is stress-tested against interest rate changes, cooling measures, and market cycles.

3

Precision Execution

Timing is everything. I orchestrate the sale, purchase, and financing sequence to minimise ABSD exposure, maximise loan quantum, and protect your cash reserves.

4

Ongoing Portfolio Management

Our relationship does not end at the transaction. I continuously monitor your portfolio against market movements and advise on the optimal time for your next strategic move.
