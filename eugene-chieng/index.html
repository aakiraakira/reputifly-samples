About Me

Property Is Not Just About Buying & Selling

I'm Eugene Chieng — a Singapore-based realtor with more than 15 years of experience across both residential and commercial property. Real estate has always been part of my life. I come from a family of realtors and I'm a second-generation agent.

I've personally stepped away for a few years to build my own private equity investments, and that experience changed how I see property — not just as an asset, but as a wealth-building tool.

Now, I bring that same mindset to help my clients grow. It's about using the right strategy to upgrade, restructure, and build wealth over time.
