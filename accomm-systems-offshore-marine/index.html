Deep capability

Marine HVAC engineered for Zone-1 & Zone-2 hazardous areas.

From refrigerant conversions to full plant retrofits, our HVAC&R team integrates fire-rated boundaries, NFPA 496 pressurisation and customised PLC control — designed for harsh offshore conditions without overspecifying capital.

  • AHU, FCU, chiller and condensing unit overhaul
  • A0 & A60 fire-rated boundary compliance
  • R407C, R134a and R22 refrigerant conversions
  • Customised Preventative Maintenance Agreements
