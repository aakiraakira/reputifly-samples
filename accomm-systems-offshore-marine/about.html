Who we are

Built for the harshest environments at sea.

Accomm Systems Offshore & Marine Pte Ltd is a Singapore-based specialist in turnkey accommodation quarters, marine engineering and ship chandelling — supporting operators across Asia.

ASOM was established to consolidate the engineering, fabrication and chandelling capabilities required to deliver offshore living quarter projects end-to-end. We work directly with shipowners, EPC contractors and shipyards to design, fabricate and install accommodation modules for semi-submersible platforms, drill ships, rigs and vessels.

We are an affiliate of Swift De Marine Pte Ltd, with an active project footprint across Singapore, Indonesia, Malaysia, Thailand and India.

Vision

Transforming spaces with cutting-edge design and sustainable craftsmanship — creating environments where ocean-goers thrive.

Mission

Empowering with innovative, reliable and sustainable solutions to enhance safety, efficiency and operational excellence.

Operating principles

Efficiency with safety · Sustainability in craftsmanship · Operational excellence · Cutting-edge in design.
