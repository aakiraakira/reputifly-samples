Common Questions

Here are the most common questions we get from our clients. We believe in full transparency before we start working together.

Still have questions?

Can't find the answer you're looking for? Please chat to our friendly team.

How long does onboarding take?

Typically less than 48 hours. Once you sign the proposal, we'll send over a welcome packet and schedule our kickoff call immediately.

Do you work with startups?

Yes! We have a specific package designed for early-stage companies that focuses on high-impact, low-cost strategies to get you off the ground.

What is your refund policy?

We offer a full refund if you are not satisfied with our initial strategy session. After project work begins, refunds are handled on a case-by-case basis as outlined in your contract.

Who will be my point of contact?

You will be assigned a dedicated Account Manager who will be your main point of contact for all day-to-day communications and weekly reporting.
