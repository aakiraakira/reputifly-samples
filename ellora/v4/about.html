About Ellora Furnishings

Elevating Singaporean living spaces with bespoke, premium furnishings since 2012.

Our Story

Ellora Furnishings was born out of a simple observation: Singaporean homeowners needed a solution that allowed them to enjoy their balconies and semi-outdoor spaces regardless of the unpredictable tropical weather.

What started as a modest operation providing custom curtains has evolved into a premier destination for motorized zip blinds and bespoke window solutions. For over a decade, we have been at the forefront of introducing smart, weather-resistant furnishings to thousands of homes across the island.

Our commitment remains unchanged: to merge functional protection with uncompromising aesthetic elegance.
