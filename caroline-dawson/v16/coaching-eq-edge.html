Commonly Asked Questions

Key information to help you prepare for your EQ journey.

Is EQ actually a skill that can be "taught"?

Absolutely. Unlike IQ, which is relatively static, EQ is a set of competencies that can be measured and improved. Our experts focus on "neuro-plasticity" — using deliberate practice to rewire your emotional responses for better professional outcomes.

How do we measure the ROI of an EQ workshop?

We look at "Cultural Health Indicators." Organizations that prioritize EQ see a measurable decrease in internal friction, an increase in employee engagement scores, and more effective cross-functional collaboration.

Is this workshop "too touchy-feely" for a corporate environment?

Not at all. We treat EQ as Performance Engineering. Our approach is data-driven and focused on the "Hard Science" of emotions — how they impact decision-making, productivity, and the bottom line.

How does this integrate with profiling tools like DISC or MBTI?

EQ is the application of that self-awareness. While DISC tells you your style, EQ Amplification gives you the "steering wheel" to adjust that style in real-time to meet the emotional needs of your team.

Can this be delivered to a global, diverse leadership team?

Yes. EQ is a universal human language. Our experts are highly experienced in facilitating sessions for multicultural groups, ensuring that the concepts of empathy and influence are applied with cultural sensitivity and local relevance.
