Our Core Methodologies

The Four-Pillar Framework

Methodology I

The GROW Model

The Foundation of Goal-Directed Action — the gold standard for structured coaching, used to clarify aspirations and establish a concrete path toward achievement.

  • G — Goal: Defining the specific achievement for the session.
  • R — Reality: Assessing current resources and hurdles.
  • O — Options: Brainstorming diverse pathways to success.
  • W — Will: Securing the commitment to act.
Methodology II

The OSKAR Model

Solution-Focused Coaching — a framework that shifts the focus away from problems and toward building on existing strengths and successes.

  • O — Outcome: Establishing the desired future state.
  • S — Scale: Quantifying the current gap.
  • K — Know-How: Identifying the skills and attributes required.
  • A — Affirm + Action: Validating current strengths and taking the next step.
  • R — Review: Evaluating progress at the start of every session.
Methodology III

The CLEAR Model

Transformational Leadership Coaching — designed to foster deep engagement and psychological safety, ensuring sessions are transformative rather than transactional.

  • C — Contract: Setting clear expectations for the coaching relationship.
  • L — Listen: Creating a safe, supportive space for inquiry.
  • E — Explore: Uncovering underlying motivations and perspectives.
  • A — Action: Defining the strategies for growth.
  • R — Review: Celebrating successes and identifying future development areas.
Methodology IV

The SMART Model

The Science of Precision — the essential foundation for execution, ensuring that every strategic insight is translated into a clear, trackable, and high-impact objective.

  • S — Specific: Eliminating ambiguity.
  • M — Measurable: Establishing clear tracking criteria.
  • A — Achievable: Ensuring goals are realistic and attainable.
  • R — Relevant: Aligning goals with the broader business strategy.
  • T — Time-Bound: Creating a healthy sense of urgency with firm deadlines.
