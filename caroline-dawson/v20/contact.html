Frequently Asked Questions

Your Strategic Questions, Answered

The questions L&D leaders most often ask before partnering with CDI — clarified upfront so you can plan with confidence.

What makes the CDI training approach different from other corporate workshops?

The "CDI Difference" lies in our proprietary S.M.A.R.T. Framework. While many programs focus on theory, we prioritize Practicality and Active-Learning. Led by Dr. Caroline Dawson, our sessions are high-energy "interactive laboratories" where participants don't just listen — they do. We blend academic rigour with real-world corporate scenarios to ensure that every minute of training translates into a tangible skill you can use the next morning.

How do your solutions align with Singapore's national "Skills-First" initiatives?

We are deeply committed to Singapore's economic transformation. Our programs are strategically mapped to the SkillsFuture Critical Core Skills (CCS) framework. By focusing on competencies like Strategic Communication, Inclusive Leadership, and Digital Fluency, we ensure that your workforce remains future-ready, agile, and aligned with national standards for professional excellence.

Which personality profiling tool is best for my team: MBTI®, DISC, Enneagram, or Lumina Spark?

The "best" tool depends on your specific goals:

  • DISC is ideal for immediate communication "flexing" and sales.
  • MBTI® is best for long-term team synergy and understanding cognitive preferences.
  • The Enneagram is perfect for deep leadership EQ and uncovering motivations.
  • Lumina Spark is our "next-gen" tool for behavioural agility and stress management.

During our initial consultation, we help you diagnose your team's current friction points to select the framework that will deliver the highest impact.

Can CDI customise workshops for specific industries or seniority levels?

Absolutely. We believe that generic training is a lost opportunity. Whether we are training C-suite executives on Executive Presence or helping mid-level managers navigate Multi-Generational Synergy, our content is "purpose-built." We conduct pre-workshop assessments to understand your specific organisational "pain points" and customise our realistic scenarios to reflect your industry's unique challenges.

How do you measure the success and ROI of your training solutions?

At CDI, success is measured by Transformation, not just attendance. We focus on "Outcome-Oriented Impact" by implementing post-workshop feedback loops and practical action plans. Our goal is to create a "ripple effect" where improved individual performance leads to measurable shifts in team productivity, psychological safety, and overall organisational culture.
