Is this a theoretical lecture or a practical workshop?

This is a clinical application session. While we leverage psychological and economic theory, our experts focus 80% of the time on applying frameworks to actual challenges your organization is currently facing.

Yes. Under strict confidentiality, we can customize the "Live Lab" portion of the workshop to use your real-world strategic dilemmas, ensuring you walk away with a solved problem, not just a learned theory.

We cover a curated selection of gold-standard models, including the Cynefin Framework for complexity, Game Theory for competitive positioning, and Cognitive Bias Audits for objective evaluation.

Strategic Decision-Making is the "operating system" for leadership. It complements our MBTI or DISC sessions by taking the self-awareness gained there and applying it to external, high-stakes organizational choices.

Is this suitable for the entire management team?

Absolutely. While it is designed for C-suite and Senior VP levels, it is highly effective for any "High-Potential" leadership cohort that is responsible for P&L or long-term strategic planning.
