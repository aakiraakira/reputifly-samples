Commonly Asked Questions

Key information to help you prepare for your executive diagnostic.

Which psychometric instruments will I be assessed against?

We typically deploy a curated combination of MBTI, DISC, the Enneagram, and Lumina Spark, calibrated to your specific role and strategic context. The exact battery is determined during the intake conversation so the instruments selected align directly with your KPIs and organizational pressures.

How is confidentiality protected during the 360-degree feedback process?

Every stakeholder interview is conducted under strict anonymity protocols. Feedback is aggregated and de-identified before being presented to you, so peers, direct reports, and board members can be candid without fear of attribution. Raw responses never leave our team.

What does the 90-minute debrief actually look like?

The debrief is a structured working session, not a lecture. We walk through the data layer by layer, isolate the two or three behaviors with the highest organizational leverage, and end with a personalized 90-day action plan. You leave with a concrete roadmap, not just insights.

Is this suitable for a newly appointed C-suite leader?

Yes — the Leadership Blueprint is especially valuable during the first 100 days of a new C-suite role. It establishes an objective baseline of how you are perceived, identifies the cultural code of the new organization, and helps you calibrate your leadership style for maximum early impact.

Can the engagement be extended into ongoing coaching?

Absolutely. The Leadership Blueprint frequently serves as the diagnostic gateway to a multi-month executive coaching engagement. Many clients combine it with The Decision Advantage and The EQ Edge to build a complete executive mastery programme.
