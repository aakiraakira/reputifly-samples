About OFC Furniture

Singapore's trusted one-stop office furniture supplier — delivering quality, innovation, and excellence since 2009.

Who We Are

Your Trusted Office Furniture Partner in Singapore

OFC Furniture focuses on providing quality solutions in the furnishing of office spaces. We offer a diverse range of workstations, office seating, storage solutions, and designer furniture that can turn your office into the most conducive working space.

Customised to your needs, OFC Furniture is the spark for organization, productivity and innovation. We believe that the right office environment can significantly impact employee well-being, creativity, and overall business performance.

With over 15 years of experience serving businesses across Singapore, we understand the unique requirements of modern workspaces. Our team of experts works closely with you from initial consultation to final installation, ensuring every piece of furniture perfectly fits your space and budget.
