FAQ

Common Questions

Do I need to make an appointment to visit your showroom?+
While walk-ins are welcome during our operating hours, we recommend making an appointment so our team can prepare and provide you with personalized attention. Contact us at +65 9883 8858 to schedule a visit.
How long does it take to receive a quotation?+
We typically provide quotations within 1-2 business days after understanding your requirements. For complex projects requiring site assessment, it may take up to 3-5 business days.
Do you provide site assessment and space planning?+
Yes! We offer complimentary site assessments for projects in Singapore. Our team will visit your office to assess the space, take measurements, and recommend optimal furniture layouts tailored to your needs.
What areas in Singapore do you deliver to?+
We deliver and install office furniture across all areas of Singapore, including the CBD, industrial estates, and business parks. Delivery and installation are typically included in your quotation.
Can I customize the furniture to match our office branding?+
Absolutely! We offer extensive customization options including choice of fabrics, laminates, color finishes, and configurations. Our team can help you select options that align perfectly with your brand identity.
