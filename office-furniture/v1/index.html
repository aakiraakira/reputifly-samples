Frequently Asked Questions

What types of office furniture does OFC provide?+
OFC Furniture offers a comprehensive range including office workstations, panel system workstations, meeting and discussion tables, modular storage cabinets, office seating (ergonomic chairs), and custom designer furniture tailored to your workspace needs.
Do you offer free consultation and space planning?+
Yes! We provide complimentary consultation and physical assessment of your workspace. Our team will create an optimal furniture layout plan and help you select the right pieces for your office environment.
Can office furniture be customized to our branding?+
Absolutely. We offer extensive customization options including choice of fabrics, color finishes for work tops, cabinets, and legs structures, as well as fabric panel selections to match your brand identity and office aesthetics.
What is the delivery and installation timeline?+
We pride ourselves on timely delivery. Standard orders are typically delivered within 2-4 weeks. Our professional installation team ensures minimal disruption to your daily operations.
Do you provide after-sales support and warranty?+
Yes, all our office furniture comes with warranty coverage. We also provide dedicated after-sales support to keep your furniture in top condition and address any concerns promptly.
