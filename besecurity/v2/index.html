About BeSecurity

Licensed Cybersecurity Expertise for Modern Organizations

A spin-off from a leading Israeli Cyber Security Company - Founded in 2012, Besecurity Pte Ltd is a licensed cybersecurity firm headquartered in Singapore. We are authorized by the Cyber Security Regulations Office under Singapore's Cybersecurity Act 2018 to operate Security Operations Centres and conduct vulnerability assessment and penetration testing services.

We prioritize safeguarding your organization's digital assets with comprehensive cybersecurity solutions that adapt to evolving threats. We combine best-of-breed technology with local expertise to deliver services that do not just detect threats—we anticipate them.
