What is Deer Placenta?

Deer placenta is a natural ingredient derived from deer, used in dietary supplement formulations. Scientific literature describes it as containing naturally occurring proteins, peptides, amino acids, and other biological constituents including growth factors. It has a long history in traditional wellness practices, particularly in East Asian cultures, where it has been valued for centuries. Modern supplement formulations use concentrated extracts, typically in softgel capsule format for improved delivery.
