Frequently Asked Questions

Common inquiries regarding our diving and salvage operations.

What is your emergency response time for distressed vessels?

We maintain a 24/7 state of readiness. Depending on the vessel's location within the Straits, our advance assessment team can typically mobilize and be on-site within 4-6 hours, with heavy salvage equipment following shortly after.

Are your commercial divers certified for civil engineering works?

Yes, our team holds full commercial diving certifications (IMCA/HSE equivalents) and specialized qualifications for underwater welding, NDT inspections, and civil marine construction.

What depths are you equipped to operate in?

We handle surface-supplied air diving for standard operations and employ mixed-gas diving and ROV (Remotely Operated Vehicle) support for deeper or more complex subsea interventions. Contact us for specific depth requirements.

Do you handle hazardous cargo recovery?

Yes. We have strict protocols for environmental protection and hazardous material containment during submerged cargo retrieval. We work closely with maritime authorities to ensure full compliance during these delicate operations.
