Our Story

Building Expertise Beneath the Surface

The journey of Straits Diving & Engineering (SDE) began long before our formal incorporation. Our core dive team first began working together in 2002, building a foundation of trust and technical synergy over fifteen years of hands-on experience. Comprised of veteran commercial divers with extensive expertise in delivering underwater services for both government and commercial sectors, we officially established SDE in 2017 to continue our specialized mission in the marine industry.

Since our official founding in Singapore, we have successfully delivered a wide range of underwater services across countries, Singapore, West Malaysia, Vietnam, Bangladesh and lastly, Mozambique, Africa. Our portfolio is diverse, spanning from underwater inspections and maintenance to new marine construction support and emergency salvage operations. Whether it is the salvage of work barges or the execution of stone revetment and coastal protection projects, our team provides unparalleled support to the marine construction sector.

At Straits Diving & Engineering, we pride ourselves on a responsive service model backed by deep technical expertise. Headquartered in Singapore with our registered office at Lower Delta Road, and supported by our strategic branch in Johor, Malaysia, our operations are powered by a dedicated regional team. We uphold the highest standards of safety, quality and performance, ensuring that every project meets or exceeds the expectations of our clientele.

Foundation

Established in Singapore as a specialist commercial diving company serving the local maritime industry.

Regional Expansion

In March 2019, Straits Diving & Engineering Sdn. Bhd. was incorporated in Malaysia to strengthen our regional presence and better support opportunities within the Malaysian marine industry.

ISO Certification

Achieved ISO 9001 and ISO 14001 certifications, reinforcing our commitment to quality and environmental stewardship.

Fleet Modernization

Invested in state-of-the-art ROV systems, diving support vessels, and subsea construction equipment.

ASEAN Coverage

Now operating across 8 Southeast Asian countries with 24/7 emergency response capability throughout the region.
