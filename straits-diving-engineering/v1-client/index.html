About Us

Decades of Experience 

Straits Diving & Engineering is a Singapore-based commercial diving company specializing in underwater construction and engineering. With our headquarters in Singapore and a branch in Johor, Malaysia, we bring a team of certified divers and skilled engineers dedicated to delivering expertise and excellence on every project.

From infrastructure to steel structural works projects, we deliver results that exceed expectations while maintaining the highest safety standards in the industry.
