The Legacy of Dr. Napoleon Hill

20 Years of Research. 504 Extraordinary Individuals. One Proven Formula.

Dr. Napoleon Hill (1883 – 1970)

Each of the 504 successful men and women averaged 50 years of experience spanning all of American business, industry, and the arts. Combined collectively, this represents 25,000 years of experience through trial and error. From this monumental research, Dr. Napoleon Hill formulated the Science of Success.

This endeavor required the collaboration of over 500 of America's most successful figures who had achieved fame and fortune through their accomplishments — including Theodore Roosevelt, Henry Ford, Thomas Edison, Alexander Graham Bell, John D. Rockefeller, and many more.

Whatever the mind can conceive and believe, it can achieve.

— Napoleon Hill
