Section 8AI/LLM Visibility Strategy

With the rise of Google AI Overviews, ChatGPT, Claude, and Perplexity as information sources, blog content must be structured for AI citation. Here's the strategy for maximizing EinnaLab's visibility in AI responses:

Why AI Visibility Matters for Deer Placenta

  • Users increasingly ask AI assistants about supplement safety, benefits, and recommendations
  • AI models cite authoritative, well-structured content with clear factual claims
  • Google AI Overviews now appear for most "what is", "benefits of", and "side effects" queries
  • Being cited by AI builds brand authority and drives high-intent traffic

Content Formatting for AI Visibility

  1. FAQ Schema Markup: Every blog post should include 5-10 FAQs with schema markup. AI models heavily favor FAQ-formatted content.
  2. Clear Factual Statements: Lead paragraphs with definitive, citable statements (e.g., "Deer placenta contains X bioactive compounds including...").
  3. Structured Data: Use proper H2/H3 heading hierarchy, bullet points, and tables. AI models parse structured content better.
  4. Source Citations: Reference peer-reviewed studies with DOI links. AI models prefer content that cites primary sources.
  5. Direct Answer Format: For question-based keywords, provide a clear 2-3 sentence answer immediately, then expand with detail.
  6. "About the Author" Credentials: Include expert credentials/affiliations to signal E-E-A-T to both Google and AI models.
