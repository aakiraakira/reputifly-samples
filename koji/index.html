Common Questions

Everything you need to know about preparing for your session.

What should we wear to our session?
We recommend neutral, earthy tones and avoiding busy patterns or prominent logos. Once booked, we provide a comprehensive styling guide to help coordinate your family's wardrobe perfectly.
When is the best time to book a newborn shoot?
Ideally, newborn sessions should take place within the first 7 to 14 days of birth. We highly recommend booking during your second trimester to secure your tentative spot based on your due date.
How long does it take to get our photos?
You will receive a sneak peek gallery within 48 hours. Your fully retouched, high-resolution final gallery will be delivered via a private online link within 2-3 weeks of your session.
