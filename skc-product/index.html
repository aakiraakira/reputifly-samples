Wine Profile

On the nose, expect an intoxicating bouquet of blackcurrant, dark plum, and violets, underpinned by subtle notes of cedar and crushed rock. The palate delivers a cascade of dark fruit flavors, seamlessly integrated with fine-grained tannins and a bright core of acidity, leading to a complex, savory finish.
Sourced exclusively from our high-elevation estate blocks. The rocky, volcanic soils provide exceptional drainage, forcing the vines to dig deep for nutrients. This struggle results in remarkably concentrated grapes with a distinct mineral character that defines our signature style.
An ideal companion for rich, savory dishes. Pair this robust red with a dry-aged ribeye steak, braised short ribs, wild mushroom risotto, or aged hard cheeses like Parmigiano-Reggiano and sharp Cheddar.
