At Oriental System Interior, our mission is to redefine the Singaporean renovation experience. We envision a landscape where high-quality craftsmanship meets absolute transparency, ensuring every homeowner can achieve their dream space without the stress of hidden fees or middleman markups.

Our Story

Oriental System Interior was founded on a simple principle: to bring factory-direct quality straight to homeowners. Born out of a deep-rooted passion for carpentry and structural integrity, we recognized a flaw in the traditional interior design industry—too many middlemen driving up costs and diluting communication.

By establishing our own in-house fabrication facility and assembling a team of HDB-licensed professionals, we cut out the intermediaries. Over the years, this direct approach has allowed us to deliver hundreds of successful BTO, Resale, and Commercial projects, earning us a reputation for reliability, speed, and uncompromising quality.

Our Core Philosophy

Absolute Transparency

We believe in honest, itemized quoting. What you see on the proposal is what you pay—no hidden fees, no sudden material surcharges.

Direct Accountability

With our own in-house carpentry factory and licensed workers, we control the timeline and quality directly, ensuring promises are actually kept.

Enduring Quality

We don't cut corners on materials behind the walls. We build spaces designed to withstand the rigors of daily life for decades.
