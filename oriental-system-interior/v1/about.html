We are a Singapore-based interior design and renovation firm dedicated to transforming homes with craftsmanship you can trust, transparency you can count on, and quality that stands the test of time.

Our Story

Built on Honesty, Driven by Craft

Oriental System Interior was founded with a clear mission: to offer homeowners in Singapore an interior design experience rooted in trust, accountability, and exceptional workmanship. From the very beginning, we set out to do things differently.

Unlike most firms that outsource to subcontractors, we operate our own in-house carpentry workshop and employ a dedicated team of designers, carpenters, and project managers. This means every detail, from initial concept to final installation, stays under our watchful eye.

Our in-house fabrication process ensures tighter quality control, faster turnaround times, and a level of consistency that outsourced work simply cannot match. Every joint, every finish, every edge is built to our exacting standards.

Our Core Philosophy

The Principles That Guide Every Project

Three foundational values shape every decision we make and every space we create.

Absolute Transparency

No hidden costs, no surprise charges. We provide fully itemised quotations so you know exactly where every dollar goes. Our clients are partners, not transactions, and trust is the foundation of every relationship we build.

Direct Accountability

Because we don't outsource, there's no finger-pointing and no runaround. Our in-house team handles every phase of your project, from design to carpentry to installation. One team, one standard, one point of responsibility.

Enduring Quality

We use premium materials and proven construction techniques to deliver interiors that last. Our own carpentry workshop allows us to maintain exacting standards on every cut, joint, and finish, ensuring nothing leaves our facility unless it's perfect.
